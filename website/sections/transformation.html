Transformation

Saving and restoring
Saving and restoring pushes and pops transformations from a stack. The methods can be nested 100 times before the stack runs out of memory. 

void save();
void restore();

Translate
Moves the origo by 'x' and 'y' pixels. 

void translate(float x, float y);

Rotate
Rotates the coordinate system by 'angle' degrees. 

void rotate(float angle);

Scale
Scales the coordinate system by a factor of 'x' and 'y'. 

void scale(float x, float y);
